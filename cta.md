## 🧠 Marketing Psychology & CRO Principles Used
To ensure maximum engagement and zero robotic tone, these CTAs rely on 4 advanced copywriting frameworks:
1. **Value-First Framing:** Replacing restrictive micro-actions with clear experiential outcomes.
2. **Micro-Urgency:** Spiking instant action by highlighting fresh morning batches and local availability.
3. **The Low-Friction Entry:** Making physical visits and digital ordering sound completely effortless.
4. **Social Affinity:** Encouraging community tagging and sharing to drive organic, algorithmic reach.
