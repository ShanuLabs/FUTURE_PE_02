### Task 2: AI Content Marketing using UGC Ads

**Brand:** Brew Haven Café  
**Theme:** Premium Specialty Coffee & Artisan Bakery

---

## 🛠️ Video Production Guidelines for Creators
* **Aspect Ratio:** Vertical 9:16 (optimized for mobile screens like Reels & Shorts).
* **Filming Style:** Raw smartphone footage, slight macro camera movements, aesthetic natural lightning.
* **Text Overlays:** Use native platform bold fonts for the initial hooks to ensure high retention.

---

### 💻 Script 1: The Remote Worker / Freelancer Escape
* **Target Audience:** Freelancers, Remote Workers, Tech Professionals in Patna.
* **Psychology Trigger:** The "Third Place" Effect (escaping work-from-home burnout).
* **On-Screen Text (Hook Overlay):** Patna’s ultimate remote workstation 💻

| Scene | Shot |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Close-up of a creator sighing at a messy desk at home with papers scattered everywhere. |
| **2** | Sharp match-cut to opening a sleek laptop at a beautiful minimal wooden café table. |
| **3** | Fast cinematic macro cuts of a condensation-covered glass of premium layered Cold Brew. |
| **4** | Wide pan of the smooth lofi ambiance, warm lighting, and green indoor plants. |
| **5** | Creator taking a satisfying sip, typing peacefully, and pointing towards the frame. |

* **Voiceover (Natural Cadence):**  
"Okay, working from home in Patna was officially killing my focus. So I moved my workstation here to Brew Haven Café in Boring Road. The high-speed Wi-Fi is seamless, the ambient music is pure focus bliss, and their signature Flat White keeps me sharp all day. If you’re a coder or creator, this is your sign to reset your routine. Drop by and claim your table today."
