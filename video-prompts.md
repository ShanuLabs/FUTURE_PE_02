## 🧠 Algorithmic Framework for Video Prompts
To command next-generation video synthesis models to output smooth, structurally coherent, and cinematic clips without warping or artifacts, every prompt follows a strict engineering formula:
1. **Camera Movement & Speed:** Explicit cinematography tracking commands (e.g., slow-motion macro pan, smooth gimbal glide).
2. **Core Subject & Kinetic Action:** Detailed description of fluid or organic human movements.
3. **Lighting Setup & Color Palette:** Warm amber tones, cinematic volumetrics, and natural glass reflections.
4. **Environment Context:** High-end, minimal architectural details matching Patna's premium local café aesthetic.

## 🛠️ Execution Manual for Video Models
* **Motion Dynamics Tuning:** In models like Runway Gen-3, keep the motion velocity parameter low (between `2` and `4`) to avoid sudden spatial distortion or morphing in highly detailed elements like latte art and croissant texture cracks.
* **Aspect Optimization:** Ensure the aspect engine is strictly locked to vertical formatting (`9:16`) inside the prompt code parameters to align perfectly with standard native Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts UI grids.
