## 🧠 Engineering Structure for Visual Prompts
To extract elite, commercial-grade imagery from state-of-the-art AI models, every prompt in this pack is structured using professional photographic parameters:
1. **Core Subject & Action:** Clear definition of the premium café elements.
2. **Environment & Framing:** Aesthetic, minimalist interior design matching the brand persona.
3. **Lighting & Atmosphere:** Warm, soft cinematic lighting to invoke a cozy "third-place" emotion.
4. **Technical Render Tokens:** Specific lens metrics, composition styles, and aspect ratios to guide the diffusion model's render pipeline.

## 🛠️ Execution Manual for Visual Assets
* **Model Configuration:** For Midjourney v6, always keep the `--stylize` parameter between `100` and `250` to prevent the model from over-complicating the clean, minimalist layout designs.
* **Color Constancy Control:** To keep your visual grid perfectly matching your online landing page, manually input hexadecimal color descriptors (like `warm cream backgrounds`, `neutral beige accents`, or `dark timber furniture`) if any specific model over-saturates the color space.
